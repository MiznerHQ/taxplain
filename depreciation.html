What is Depreciation (Section 179)?

Deduction
⚠️ Educational only. TaxPlain does not provide tax, legal, or financial advice. Always consult a qualified tax professional about your specific situation.
What it is

The Depreciation (Section 179) the process of deducting the cost of business assets over time — Section 179 allows immediate expensing of qualifying purchases.

Who it affects & common mistakes
👤 Who it affects

Business owners and self-employed people who purchase equipment, vehicles, machinery, or other business assets.

⚠️ Common mistake

Forgetting to claim depreciation on business assets. Depreciation is a significant deduction that many small business owners overlook or under-claim.

What it means for you

Instead of deducting a large purchase over many years, Section 179 lets you deduct the full cost in the year of purchase, up to $1,160,000 for 2023. Bonus depreciation allows additional immediate deductions.

What to do right now
Keep detailed records of all business asset purchases including the date, cost, and business use percentage. A tax professional can help you decide between Section 179, bonus depreciation, and regular depreciation for maximum tax benefit.
